# Honey Mustard Sauce
## Delicious on mixed salad and sandwiches

### Prep: 5mins

### Ingredients

- 2 tbsp. crème fraîche or sour cream
- 2 tsp. honey
- 2 tsp. mild mustard
- 2 tbsp. oil
- 1 tbsp. (red wine) vinegar
- pepper, salt

### Directions

1. Mix everything together
2. salt to taste
3. profit!
4. Add 2 tbsp. oil extra if you want to use it as salad dressing
