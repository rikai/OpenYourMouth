# Fried bacon
## The nice but unhealthy way to bacon

### Ingredients

- Bacon
- Oil

### Steps

1. Check if bacon is frozen
	- (true) defrost bacon
		- See [defrosting bacon](DefrostingBacon.md)
	- (false) continue
2. Separate bacon into slices
3. Heat frying pan
4. Add oil
	- Not to much!
5. Put the bacon on the pan
6. Fry until cooked
7. Take bacon off pan
8. Eat
