# Grilled bacon
## Healthy bacon

### Ingredients

- Bacon

## Steps

1. Check if bacon is frozen
	- (true) Defrost bacon
		- See [defrosting bacon](DefrostingBacon.md)
	- (false) Continue
2. Separate into slices
3. Turn on grill
4. Place on grill
5. Grill bacon
	- Turn bacon half way through to more evenly cook both sides
	- Until bacon is crispy
6. Take bacon off grill
	- Turn grill off!
7. Eat
